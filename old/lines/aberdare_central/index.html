Aberdare Central Branch

  • History

This line left the Cessnock Branch a few kilometres to the east of Cessnock, and served the Aberdare Central Colliery. The line was built in 1917 and served the colliery until it closed in November 1961. The line was left intact for many years, although it was cannibalised in the early 1970s.
