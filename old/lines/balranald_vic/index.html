Balranald Line

  • History

The Balranald line runs from the far south-west town of Balranald to Barnes Junction where it meets the Deniliquin line.

It is a broad gauge line operated and maintained by Victoria. Primary traffic is rice and wheat. Currently, trains only run as far as Moulamein.
