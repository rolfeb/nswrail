Current status: The ornate truncated Down Home signal post is still present. Just beyond is the overgrown remains of the platform, including the lever frame. Beyond the station is the partially-filled turntable pit. On the up side of the yard are the jib crane and several goods sheds and loading banks. There are several silos nearby.
