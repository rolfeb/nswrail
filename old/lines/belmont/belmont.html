Belmont Railway Station

 John Darling Platform
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Belmont Branch
Location: Lat/long: (151.6610°, -33.0388°)
Distance: From Sydney: 176.620 km
From Adamstown: 15.50 km
History:
2-Jan-1917   Opened
9-Apr-1971   Closed
Description: A 96m up-side platform with a run-around siding and goods facilities. To the south of the station were sidings used to load coal from the nearby Wallermaine and Normaine, and Belmont Collieries.
Current status: Only the remains of the wooden-faced platform are visible under very thick overgrowing vegetation at the intersection of Railway and Ernest Streets. The one-time coal loading sidings are now under part of the Belmont Institute of Technology.
