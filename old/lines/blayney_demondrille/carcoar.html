Description: By 1919, Carcoar consisted of a station with engine dock at the south end. There was a loop siding, a goods siding and shed, and a stock siding with stock yard.
Current status: Recently painted in heritage colours, the station is still present. The loop and siding are still present, the latter containing an old water gin.
