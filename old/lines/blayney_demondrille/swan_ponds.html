History:
1908   Opened
29-Jun-1974   Closed
Description: By 1918 Swan Ponds was a crossing loop with platforms on both the up and down lines. Each end of the loop featured a water tank and runaway siding, with the up line also having an ash pit.
Current status: The remnants of the old loop and station buildings still remain.
