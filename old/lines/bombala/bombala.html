History:
21-Nov-1921   Opened
unknown   Closed
Description: The station and 88m platform were on the up side of the line. Directly opposite were a runaround loop and goods siding, the latter featuring a loading bank, goods shed and gantry crane. A footbridge allowed pedestrians access to the platform and across the yard. North of the station was an up stock siding with stock yards, and on the other side was the Loco Road, leading to the coal stage and turntable.
Current status: The station and yard are essentially intact. The footbridge over the station is still present, although access to the platform is blocked. Several buildings are scattered around the yard, including what appears to be some kind of forge. The turntable is still present lies at the northern end.
