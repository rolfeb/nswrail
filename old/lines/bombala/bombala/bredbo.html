Bredbo Railway Station

Colinton Tunnel Billilingra
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Distance: From Sydney: 399.79 km
From Joppa Junction: 169.30 km
Description: Bredbo station sat on an 80m platform on the down side of the main line. Opposite the station were a loop line and stock siding with stock yards. Also on the down side was a goods siding with a small shed.
Current status: The platform and station building is still present, although currently unused.
