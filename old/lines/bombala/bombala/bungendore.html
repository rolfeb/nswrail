Bungendore Railway Station

Butmaroo Bungendore Junction
Main facility: Station
Status: In Use
Distance: From Sydney: 293.50 km
From Joppa Junction: 63.01 km
Description: An important location on the line, Bungendore station included a 130m up side platform with a dock at the up end. At the down end were several sidings with a goods shed and loading bank. Opposite this was a triangle for turning locomotives. By 1984 the south fork of the triangle had been lifted.
