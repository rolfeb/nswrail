Jincumbilly Railway Station

Holts Flat Bukalong
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Distance: From Sydney: 510.03 km
From Joppa Junction: 279.54 km
Description: Featured a 120m platform with shelter on the down side of the main line. Opposite was a loop siding with sheep yards.
Current status: The heavily-truncated platform and station building remain. Next to the platform is the remains of 2 louvered wagons (bogies and other fittings removed). The line out of Jincumbilly has been obliterated by the main road.
