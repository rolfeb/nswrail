Tarago Railway Station

Lake Bathurst Mount Fairy
Main facility: Station
Status: In Use
Distance: From Sydney: 262.51 km
From Joppa Junction: 32.02 km
Description: An 84m platform, with a dock at the down end of the station. Opposite the station was a passing loop. At the up end of the station was a stock siding, and at the down end were a pair of goods sidings with a goods shed. In later years, another siding was added beyond the loop line, for the use of the nearby Woodlawn Mine.
