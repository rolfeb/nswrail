Bukalong Railway Station

 Jincumbilly Bombala 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Bombala Line
Location: Lat/long: (149.2326°, -36.8031°)
Distance: From Sydney: 519.520 km
From Joppa Junction: 289.03 km
History:
21-Nov-1921   Opened
9-Jul-1962   Closed
Description: A 57m platform with shelter sat on the down side of the line. Opposite was a goods siding loading to a loading bank and stock yards.
Current status: The station and platform remain, although part of the roof has collapsed. The goods bank is present, although the goods siding has mostly been removed. The platform roads has had 2 short sections of rail removed. Adjacent to the goods line is a cattle loader in good condition.
