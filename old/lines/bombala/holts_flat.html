Holts Flat Railway Station

 Maclaughlin Jincumbilly 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Bombala Line
Location: Lat/long: (149.2752°, -36.6385°)
Distance: From Sydney: 493.560 km
From Joppa Junction: 263.07 km
History:
21-Nov-1921   Opened
9-Mar-1975   Closed
Description: The 88m platform and shelter stood on the up side of the line, opposite a loop siding.
Current status: The truncated platform and small building remain in reasonable condition. The area is used for grazing, and thus the yard is well cleared. The platform road and one siding still remain. A 2 lever frame with associated rodding lie at the down end of the location. The remains of a gate lie at the road entrance to the locality.
