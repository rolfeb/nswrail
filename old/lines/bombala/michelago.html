Michelago Railway Station

 Williamsdale Colinton 
Main facility: Station
Status: Re-used
Lines: Bombala Line
Location: Lat/long: (149.1653°, -35.7105°)
Distance: From Sydney: 370.140 km
From Joppa Junction: 139.65 km
History:
8-Dec-1887   Opened
unknown   Closed
Description: The station building sat on a 120m platform on a platform road on the up side of the line. A water tank was located at the down end of the platform. At the south end of the station were several sidings, with stock yards, and a goods platform and shed.
Current status: A platform with well-preserved station building is present. There is also a safeworking hut on the platform. A turntable lies at the southern end. This location is the base for the Michelago Tourist Railway.
