Nimmitabel Railway Station

Coonerang Maclaughlin
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Distance: From Sydney: 473.46 km
From Joppa Junction: 242.97 km
Description: Nimmitabel station resided on an 88m platform on the down side of the main line. At the down end was a water tank and ash pit. Further down the line was a siding leading to a turntable. On the up side of the line was a crossing loop, stock siding and goods siding with loading bank and shed. By 1971, the turntable had gone.
Current status: The station building and yard are in poor condition. The area appears to be being used for storage purposes.
