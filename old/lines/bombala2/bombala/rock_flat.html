Rock Flat Railway Station

Cooma Coonerang
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Distance: From Sydney: 450.80 km
From Joppa Junction: 220.31 km
Description: Opened with the line as a platform on the down side of the line with a loop siding opposite providing access to a goods shed, loading bank and stock yards.
Current status: A platform and goods bank are still present. A rotten bridge lies just to the north. 2-lever frames mark the ends of the siding.
