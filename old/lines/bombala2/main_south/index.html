Main South Line

  • History
  • Maps

The Main South line starts from Central station and heads south to Albury on the Victorian border, and thence to Melbourne. Originally it stopped at Albury where a broad gauge line continued to Melbourne, but now it is standard gauge all the way. On the line are the major rail locations of Goulburn, Cootamundra and Junee. Much of the state's freight traffic traverses this line, as well as passenger trains.
