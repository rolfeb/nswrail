Description: Under the construction name of Burrowa, Boorowa, was commissioned on 2/2/1915 and had a 76m platform. Opposite the down-side station was the loop line, goods siding and grain siding. These served goods and grain sheds, a loading bank and gantry crane. At the up end of the station was a water tank. Further up the yard were several sidings providing access to a coal stage, turntable and engine shed.
Current status: Apart from a water column and the turntable, little else remains.
