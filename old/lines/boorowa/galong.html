Galong Railway Station

St Michaels 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Main South Line
Boorowa Branch
Location: Lat/long: (148.5573°, -34.6054°)
Distance: From Sydney: 365.200 km
History:
12-Mar-1877   Opened
9-Jun-1915   Moved site
Description: Opened on 12/3/1877, the station and the Boorowa line junction were moved during the 1916 deviation.
Current status: The station building and up platform shelter are still in good condition. The signal box is still present, and is occasionally put into use. The site of the original station and branch location are still visible.
