Gooramma Railway Station

 Oreston Boorowa 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Boorowa Branch
Location: Lat/long: (148.6221°, -34.4930°)
Distance: From Sydney: 382.563 km
From Galong: 17.36 km
History:
2-Feb-1915   Opened
5-Apr-1974   Closed
Description: A 76m platform on the up side of the line. Opposite the station was a goods siding serving a goods shed and a grain shed. The gods siding featured a 5ton gantry crane. At the down end of the location was an up-side stock siding.
Current status: The concrete floor of the grain shed is still present, along with several of the ground frames.
