Current status: The station building is being used by various local groups, including the local library. The track has been slewed away from the platform. There are 2 water towers in the yard at the up end of the station, but only the base of the water tank remains. The yard comprises a siding servicing a grain silo, and also the one-time up dock platform. There are still 2 name boards on the platform.
