Camden Railway Station

 Elderslie
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Camden Branch
Location: Lat/long: (150.6969°, -34.0507°)
Distance: From Sydney: 67.299 km
From Campbelltown: 12.59 km
History:
10-Mar-1882   Opened
10-Jan-1883   Moved site
19-Jun-1901   Moved site
1-Jan-1963   Closed
Description: The station building lay on the down side of the line, and had a run-around siding opposite. Beyond that were a pair of goods sidings with a loading bank. At the up end of the platform was a short siding leading to a goods shed. Just to the east of the yard was a siding serving the Dairy Farmers Milk Company.
Current status: The area where the station and yard were is now a Holden car dealership. The milk factory building is still present, but is now used as a Yamaha dealer.
