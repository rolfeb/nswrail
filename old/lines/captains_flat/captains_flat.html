Captains Flat Railway Station

 Hoskinstown
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Captains Flat Branch
Location: Lat/long: (149.4355°, -35.5891°)
Distance: From Sydney: 332.200 km

History:
7-Jun-1940   Opened
31-Aug-1968   Closed
8-Aug-1969   Re-opened
27-Aug-1969   Closed
Description: The station resided on a 63m curved island platform, located between the arrival and departure roads. There was also an engine run-around line and turntable, water tank and ash pit. Beyond the station area was the yard where the nearby mines would load their ore. An elevated loading ramp sat over the main line, with a goods shed on the adjacent siding.
Current status: Much of the station and surrounds is still intact although somewhat overgrown. The station, which is now privately-owned still retains its name board. The yard and loading bin (1/2 km away) are also largely intact.
