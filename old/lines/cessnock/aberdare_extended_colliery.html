Current status: Production ceased in 1973, and most structures were demolished. The area is being reforested and rehabilitated. The track has been lifted, and the only remaining structure is the "Town Lighting Team" depot, which is being used as an office for the Coal and Allied Limited Property Officer. The area is fenced off.
