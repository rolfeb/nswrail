History:
Dec-1903   Opened
unknown   Closed
Description: Consisted of a curved 160m island platform with signal box and station building. On the down side were a goods siding with shed and crane, and a loop siding.
Current status: The platform is still present, along with the foundations of the shelter and signal box. The down main is still present although not in use. A pair of restored platform signs identify the station.
