Bee Siding Railway Station

 Aberdare Junction North Kurri Kurri 
Main facility: Platform
Status: Closed
Lines: Cessnock Branch
Location: Lat/long: (151.4839°, -32.7984°)
Distance: From Sydney: 202.630 km
From Maitland Junctions: 9.63 km
Description: Bee Siding had a pair of short (30m) platforms around the main lines. On the up side was a down siding and the Soldiers Settlement siding.
Current status: Neither the platforms nor the sidings remain. Parts of the up main still remain.
