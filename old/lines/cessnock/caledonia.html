Caledonia Railway Station

 Aberdare Central Junction Aberdare Colliery 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Cessnock Branch
Location: Lat/long: (151.3844°, -32.8471°)
Distance: From Sydney: 215.670 km
From Maitland Junctions: 22.67 km
History:
16-Feb-1904   Opened as Aberdare
Nov-1912   Renamed Caledonia
unknown   Closed
Description: Consisted of a 100m island platform and station with a set of 5 colliery sidings in the down yard. There was a signal box at the up end of the platform with a water tank and column at the other end. At the Cessnock end was an up goods siding with small shed.
Current status: The platform, signal box and water tank are still present. The signal has been gutted. The water column was been removed, although the base still remains. No trace remains of the sidings.
