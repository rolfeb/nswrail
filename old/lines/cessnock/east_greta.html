History:
14-Jun-1902   Opened
unknown   Closed
Description: Opened with a pair of 80m island platforms joined at the down end, and containing the station building with adjoining signal box. A water column resided at the down end of the platform. In later years, the main lines were slewed together and a new pair of platforms constructed.
Current status: The platforms still exist on either side of the line.
