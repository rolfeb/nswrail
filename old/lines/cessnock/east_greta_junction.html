East Greta Junction Railway Station

 Maitland Junctions East Greta No 2 Colliery 
Main facility: Platform
Status: Closed
Lines: Cessnock Branch
Location: Lat/long: (151.5377°, -32.7364°)
Distance: From Sydney: 193.900 km
From Maitland Junctions: 0.90 km
Description: A pair of 70m platforms located on either side of the main lines, between the exchange sidings (at the up and) and a small yard and the loco servicing sidings (at the down end). The signal box was on the up side platform.
Current status: The platforms are still present. The signal box and various semaphore signals are still used when the occasional train traverses the line. Much of the exchange sidings have been lifted. Some of the loco servicing lines are also still present.
