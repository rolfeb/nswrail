History:
Dec-1903   Opened
unknown   Closed
Description: Weston was a 160m island platform with signal box and station building. A water tank and column were located at the down end. A footbridge provided access to the station from both sides. There was a down loop line as well as a pair of goods sidings on the up side. The main goods siding featured two loading banks and sheds as well as its own water column.
Current status: The empty platform is still present. The watering facilities have been repainted in recent times. The footbridge is still in use, although the steps to the platform have been removed.
