Craboon Railway Station

Leadville 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Gwabegar Line
Coolah Branch
Location: Lat/long: (149.4603°, -32.0294°)
Distance: From Sydney: 376.500 km
History:
26-Nov-1910   Opened
6-Apr-1975   Closed
Description: Opened with a 70m platform and station building on the down side. A up siding served stockyards, a loading bank and goods platform and shed. A gantry crane was also provided.
Current status: No trace remains of the station. The remains of the line to Coolah are still visible.
