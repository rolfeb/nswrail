Balladoran Railway Station

 Eumungerie Marthaguy 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Coonamble Branch
Location: Lat/long: (148.6524°, -31.8629°)
Distance: From Sydney: 508.470 km
From Dubbo: 46.27 km
History:
18-Feb-1903   Opened
23-Nov-1974   Closed
Description: Opened with the line as an up-side station on a 40m platform, with a loop siding opposite. The station closed in 1974.
Current status: A large tree marks the location of the station. The loop siding has been replaced with a larger dead-end siding serving a grain silo.
