Curban Railway Station

 Kamber Armatree 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Coonamble Branch
Location: Lat/long: (148.5526°, -31.5627°)
Distance: From Sydney: 544.500 km
From Dubbo: 82.30 km
History:
18-Feb-1903   Opened
23-Nov-1974   Closed
Description: Consisted of a 40m down-side platform with a crossing and goods loops opposite. The goods loop served a loading bank. The station closed in 1974.
Current status: The probably station location is a pair of large tree. A house adjacent may have been a railway residence. The original sidings have been lifted, leaving an isolated loading bank. A new loop siding serves a pair of silos.
