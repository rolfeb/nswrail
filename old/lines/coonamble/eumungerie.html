Eumungerie Railway Station

 Mogriguy Balladoran 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Coonamble Branch
Location: Lat/long: (148.6203°, -31.9485°)
Distance: From Sydney: 498.200 km
From Dubbo: 36.00 km
History:
18-Feb-1903   Opened as Coalbaggie Creek
May-1903   Renamed Eumungerie
26-Apr-1970   Closed
Description: Opened as "Coolbaggie Creek", a station with a 40m up-side platform, with a short loop opposite. A water tank was located at the down end of the loop. The station closed in 1970.
Current status: All that remains of the station is a small hut and a relocated 3-lever frame, and a low mound. The loop has been lengthened and now serves a loading bank and silos. Semaphore signals are present at each end of the loop.
