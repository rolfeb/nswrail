Mogriguy Railway Station

 Talbragar Eumungerie 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Coonamble Branch
Location: Lat/long: (148.6654°, -32.0692°)
Distance: From Sydney: 483.600 km
From Dubbo: 21.40 km
History:
18-Feb-1903   Opened
Dec-1908   Renamed Mogriguy
23-Nov-1974   Closed
Description: Opened as "Goonoo", a station with a 40m down-side platform, with a short loop siding opposite. The station closed in 1974.
Current status: A low mound is the only remains of the one-time station. The loop siding remains, serving a short loading bank and silos.
