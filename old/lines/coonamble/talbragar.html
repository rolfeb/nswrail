Talbragar Railway Station

 Troy Junction Mogriguy 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Coonamble Branch
Location: Lat/long: (148.6260°, -32.1914°)
Distance: From Sydney: 469.500 km
From Dubbo: 7.30 km
History:
18-Feb-1903   Opened
24-Nov-1974   Closed
Description: A short 24m platform on the down side of the line, with a loop siding serving a loading bank opposite. The station closed in 1974.
Current status: No trace remains of the station, although the small signboard adorns the front garden of the adjacent property. The goods bank remains; it has been raised to allow the loading of ballast from the nearby quarry. The loop siding serving the bank also now serves some silos further up the line.
