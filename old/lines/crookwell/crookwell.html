Description: Crookwell had the normal facilities for a terminal station. The station building sat on a 42m down side platform. Opposite the station was a runaround loop and and engine siding leading to a turntable and water tank. Further up the line was a stock siding with yards, and a goods siding serving a loading bank and goods shed.
Current status: Nearly all facilities are still present, including the yard, goods shed, turntable and ash pit. Only the watertank has been removed, although the foundations are still present. The station building is being maintained.
