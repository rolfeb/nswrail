Kenmore Railway Station

 Bradfordville Norwood 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Crookwell Branch
Location: Lat/long: (149.7444°, -34.7226°)
Distance: From Sydney: 229.950 km
From Goulburn North Junction: 4.19 km
History:
22-Apr-1902   Opened
4-Mar-1975   Closed
Description: An up-side station sitting on a 30m platform, with a short loop siding opposite. In 1942 the Commonwealth Siding was added beyond the loop siding. The station was closed in 1975.
Current status: The loop siding and A lever are still present. There is no trace of the Commonwealth Siding.
