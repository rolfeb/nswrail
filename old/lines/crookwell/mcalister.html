History:
22-Apr-1902   Opened
9-Mar-1975   Closed
Description: The down-side station sat on a 42m platform. Opposite was a loop siding leasing to a loading bank and goods shed. The platform was removed by 1969.
Current status: The location is on private property. The loading back is still visible from the road.
