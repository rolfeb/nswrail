Roslyn Railway Station

 Prells Siding McAlister 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Crookwell Branch
Taralga Branch
Location: Lat/long: (149.6088°, -34.5003°)
Distance: From Sydney: 264.650 km
From Goulburn North Junction: 38.89 km
History:
22-Apr-1902   Opened
21-Aug-1974   Closed
Description: A down-side station sitting on a 40m platform. Opposite was a loop siding which extended off to become the branch to Taralga. Beyond the loop was a goods siding serving a goods shed, gantry crane and loading bank. When the station opened in 1902, facilities were put in place for the Taralga branch although the branch was not actually opened until 1926. The station and facilities were removed some time after 1975.
Current status: Most of the facilities here are gone. There are no traces of the yard, and only a pair of loading banks remain to indicate there was anything at the location.
