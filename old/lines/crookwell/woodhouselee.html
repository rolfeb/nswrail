Woodhouselee Railway Station

 Graywood Prells Siding 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Crookwell Branch
Location: Lat/long: (149.6288°, -34.5680°)
Distance: From Sydney: 255.000 km
From Goulburn North Junction: 29.24 km
History:
22-Apr-1902   Opened
9-Mar-1975   Closed
Description: A down-side station sitting on a 42m platform, with a loop siding opposite. Opening with the line, it was closed in 1975.
Current status: The loop siding has been lifted, although the points levers and locks are still present at each end. There is no trace of the platform, although the collapsed remains of the shelter are still present.
