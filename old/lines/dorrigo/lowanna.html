History:
23-Dec-1924   Opened
20-Sep-1975   Closed
Description: Lowanna was the largest of the intermediate stations. It was an attended station, with a crossing loop and siding. Most of the timber was loaded at this location.
Current status: Some minor restoration work has been undertaken on the station structure and at this location the line has a small siding. Some rolling stock is present including a number of decaying wooden passenger cars and other goods wagons.
