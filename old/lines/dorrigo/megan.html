Megan Railway Station

 Briggsvale Leigh 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Dorrigo Branch
Location: Lat/long: (152.7663°, -30.2808°)
Distance: From Sydney: 707.900 km
From Glenreagh: 56.00 km
History:
23-Dec-1924   Opened
20-Sep-1975   Closed
Description: Originally consisted of a platform on the main line with a loop siding serving a loading bank and stock yards. In 1950 a crossing loop and de-ashing pit were constructed.
Current status: The small platform and shelter still remain. Rolling stock in the form of rapidly decaying wooden passenger cars and some other associated rolling stock remains.
