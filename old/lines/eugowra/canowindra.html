Canowindra Railway Station

 Bangaroo Nyrang Creek 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Eugowra Branch
Location: Lat/long: (148.6598°, -33.5587°)
Distance: From Sydney: 403.200 km
From Cowra: 37.66 km
History:
21-Sep-1910   Opened
10-Aug-1974   Closed
Description: The original terminus of the line had the normal locomotive servicing facilities, including turntable, water tank and column, ash-pit and engine shed. The latter was removed when the line was extended to Eugowra. In addition it had goods and grain sheds, a loading bank and stock yards, and a gantry crane. The station closed 10/8/1974. The site is almost completely intact, although run down. The area is maintained by the local Lions Club.
