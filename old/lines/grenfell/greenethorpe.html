Greenethorpe Railway Station

 Uppingham Brundah 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Grenfell Branch
Location: Lat/long: (148.4014°, -33.9979°)
Distance: From Sydney: 414.919 km
From Koorawatha Junction: 21.55 km
History:
7-May-1901   Opened as Iandra
15-Sep-1908   Renamed Greenethorpe
6-Apr-1975   Closed
Description: Apart the the station on the main, there was a loop line, a goods siding servicing a grain shed, goods shed and loading bank, and a grain siding which contained silos and a gantry crane. In 1981 the yard was rationalised, the the removal of the loop line and truncation of the goods siding.
Current status: These days there is no trace of the station. The silo still sees occasional wheat traffic.
