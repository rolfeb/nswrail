History:
10-Sep-1923   Opened
unknown   Closed
Description: An 87m platform stood on the down side of the line. At the down end were a water tank and column. Opposite the station were a loop line and goods siding, the latter with a loading bank. goods shed and gantry crane. At the up end of the yard was a stock siding and stock yards. Opposite these was a 200m sleeper siding. By 1985, the platform was gone, and only a single loop siding was provided, leading to some silos. One addition was a turntable off the down end of the loop.
