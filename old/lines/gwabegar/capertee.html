15-May-1882   Opened
unknown   Closed
Description: A 110m platform on the up side of the line. At the up end of the station was a water tank, whilst at the down end were three sidings with a goods shed, loading bank, stockyards and weighbridge.
Current status: The station building is being used by local groups. The platform edge has been fenced off for safety reasons (the occasional coal train still passes through). Nothing remains of the one-time yard.
