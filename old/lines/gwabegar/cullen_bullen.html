Cullen Bullen Railway Station

 Portland Baal Bone Junction 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Gwabegar Line
Location: Lat/long: (150.0067°, -33.3009°)
Distance: From Sydney: 188.310 km
From Jct (Wallerawang): 16.41 km
History:
1-May-1889   Opened as Carsons Siding and Halt
30-Jul-1896   Renamed Cullen Bullen and Platform
14-Jun-1927   Moved site
6-Mar-1976   Closed
Description: Standing on a 77m up-side platform, Cullen Bullen also possessed a loop siding. At the up end of this was an ash pit and water tank. Branching off the main near the level crossing was a 2.4km branch line to the nearby Invincible Colliery.
Current status: There is a little evidence for the old station location, apart from an old coal box at the Portland road crossing.
