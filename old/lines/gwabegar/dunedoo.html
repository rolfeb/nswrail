Dunedoo Railway Station

 Craboon Liamena 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Gwabegar Line
Location: Lat/long: (149.3959°, -32.0146°)
Distance: From Sydney: 387.900 km
From Jct (Wallerawang): 216.00 km
History:
26-Nov-1910   Opened
unknown   Closed
Description: Opened with a 70m station on the up side. Opposite were a loop line and a goods siding with a loading bank, goods shed and gantry crane. At the north end on the down side were servicing facilities consisting of a turntable, coal stage, and engine and carriage sheds. In 1935 silos and a silo siding were added. By 1965, the loco servicing facilities were gone.
Current status: Station building has been restored and painted. No platform in existence. It is believed that the platform will be restored. Layout consists of main line (platform road) and loop (silo road). Wheat silos are also in service.
