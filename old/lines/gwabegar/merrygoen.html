History:
2-Apr-1917   Opened
unknown   Closed
Description: Consisted of a station on a 90m platform on the up side. Behind the platform was a water tank which served columns at each end of the station. On the up side were a loop line, and goods and grain sidings. At the down end of the yard was a separate stock siding and sheep yards. At some point, silos and a silo siding were added.
Current status: Station building and platform are still standing. Layout consists of main line (platform road) and loop (silo road). Wheat silos are also in service.
