Description: Opened in 1911, Portland station stood on a 100m island platform. On the down side were several sidings serving a pair of loading banks, a goods shed and the nearby cement works. By 1911-1912 a private line from the Ivanhoe Colliery near Pipers Flat joined onto the cement works siding.
