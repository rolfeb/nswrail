9-Jan-1884   Opened
unknown   Closed
Description: A 90m platform on the down side of the line. At the up end was a dock platform, whilst at the other was a water tank. Opposite the station was a loop line. Branching off this loop were sidings leading to stockyards and a turntable. Next to the station were a pair of goods sidings with a goods shed.
Current status: The station building is being used as a craft shop. The signal box and levers are present, although disconnected.
