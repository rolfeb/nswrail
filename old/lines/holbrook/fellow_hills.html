Main facility: Station
Status: Closed

Location: Lat/long: (147.1617°, -35.6926°)

History:
18-Sep-1902   Opened
4-May-1975   Closed
Description: Identical layout to Morven, with of a 40m down-side platform with a loop siding and loading bank opposite. Closed on 4/5/1975.
Current status: The goods platform and siding along with the main platform are still visible. Also visible are the two-lever frames which controlled the loop.
