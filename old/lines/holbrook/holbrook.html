Holbrook Railway Station

 Ralvona
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Holbrook Branch
Location: Lat/long: (147.3147°, -35.7177°)
Distance: From Sydney: 623.000 km
From Culcairn: 26.18 km
History:
18-Sep-1902   Opened as Germanton
7-Sep-1915   Renamed Holbrook
4-May-1975   Closed
Description: Opened as Germanton, but was renamed Holbrook during the First World War. Consisted of an up side 40m platform with a loop opposite. A turntable led off the loop. Beyond the station were several sidings serving loading banks, a goods shed and stockyards.
Current status: The platform is present, although heavily overgrown. Also present are the goods platform, crane and silos.
