Hunter Valley Collieries

  • History

The upper Hunter Valley is host to a number of mines which are responsible for a large amount of the coal traffic in NSW. Each day, large trains hauled by double 90 or triple 82 locomotives haul coal down the Main North to the export terminals at Port Waratah and Kooragang Island.
