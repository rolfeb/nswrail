Kywong Branch

  • History
  • Maps

The Kywong branch was built to service the wheat crops in the south of the state. It extends from Uranquinty on the Main South Line to Kywong to the west. Given its close proximity to other lines, it would not have had much of a future, and indeed was closed in 1988.
