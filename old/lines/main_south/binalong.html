Description: The original station was replaced by a new version with the 1916 deviation.
Current status: Both the original and 1916 versions of the station still remain. The original station is being used as a residence. The new station is no longer in use. All points have been removed from the area, although the signal box still remains. Also present are the remains of sidings, water tanks and columns.
