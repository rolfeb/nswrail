History:
9-Nov-1875   Opened as Tank

9-Mar-1975   Closed
Description: Fish River was opened as Tank on 9/11/1875 and the name changed to Fish River in 1877 but was not at the current site. The station was relocated on 24/10/1915 and lasted 60 years closing on 9/3/1975. Platform lengths were a mere 52m.
