Description: Not available
Current status: This station is still in use. The closed signal box is still present, although the lever frame has been removed. Both platforms are in very good condition, and the main building is closed up, but intact. There is a shelter on the up platform. The track has a single trailing crossover and a small refuge siding on the down at the northern end of the platform. There is a rusted crane on the site of the yard, and a small maintenance depot next to the down main.
