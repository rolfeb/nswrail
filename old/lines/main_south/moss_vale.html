History:
2-Dec-1867   Opened as Sutton Forest
1877   Renamed Moss Vale
Description: Moss Vale station is a major location on the Main South line. As well as servicing a large town, it is the junction of the line to the industrial facilities at Wollongong. The station is quite unusual in that the features a very wide island platform.
