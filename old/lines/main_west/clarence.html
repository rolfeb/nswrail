History:
16-Oct-1910   Opened

Description: A pair of platforms outside of the main line. In 1958 there was a trailing siding of the up main behind the platform, but had been removed by 1977.
Current status: Little remains of the station. All that is visible is the old wooden platform facing for the former loading bank on the siding.
