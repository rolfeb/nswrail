Current status: There is an island platform with a timber station building and tin roof. The windows have been sealed with metal plating, but the building's condition is fair. There are signs denoting 'Toilets', 'Waiting Room' and 'Office' above the doorways on both sides. The platform is covered in weeds and there is a small attempt at vandalism with a few panels ripped off on the southern side. There are two unusual station signs, consisting each of 2 thin metal posts with a crossbar. A faded metal sign hangs under each with the name 'Raglan' and a spot for some sort of civic insignia, which has faded away.
