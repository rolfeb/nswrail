Denman Railway Station

 Mangoola Myambat 
Main facility: Station

Location: Lat/long: (150.6836°, -32.3858°)
Distance: From Sydney: 314.100 km

History:
26-Apr-1915   Opened
unknown   Closed
Description: Opened with the line as a station on a 100m up-side platform and substantial loco servicing facilities. At the up end of the station was a stock siding, which the down end of the yard featured an engine shed, coal stage and turntable. Opposite the station was a goods siding with shed and loading bank.
Current status: The yard is substantially intact although unused. The platform has been cut back to around 5m in length, but still sports its name board. The goods and loco facilities are gone. The elevated water tank is still present.
