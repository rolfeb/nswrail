Current status: Only a part of the wooden-faced platform, and the loading bank remain. The yard is mostly intact, although littered with debris. Only the concrete bases indicate the location of the gangers' shed, water tank and signal box. A jib crane is still present in the yard, with some silos at the down end. A fixed Home signal is still present at the south end. One of the station signs has been placed in the main street behind a memorial to Banjo Patterson.
