unknown   Closed
Description: As both the only major location on the branch, as well as the terminus, Oberon boasted a reasonable yard. Goods and loop sidings sat opposite the up-side station and 85m platform. At the up end of the yard was a siding servicing stock yards. Adjacent to the station was a siding leading to an ash-pit and turntable.
Current status: The station has been restored and now plays host to the Oberon Museum.
