Sandy Hollow - Gulgong Line

  • History
  • Maps

The Sandy Hollow-Gulgong line is in the central north of the state, and forms a cross-country link between the Main North Line and the Gwabegar Line. Interestingly, although construction commenced in 1915, the link wasn't completed until 1985.

Today, traffic on the line is dominated by coal from the Ulan colliery, hauled mainly by 90 class locomotives.
