South Coast Line

  • History
  • Maps

The South Coast line extends from the centre of Sydney south until just north of Nowra. It passes through the major city of Wollongong, which provides for plenty of passenger and freight trains. In particular, the BHP steel plant at Port Kembla requires trains for raw materials coming in, and finished products going out.
