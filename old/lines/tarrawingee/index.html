Tarrawingee Line

  • History

This narrow gauge (1067mm) line extended from Broken Hill in the state's far west, north for some 60km to the town of Tarrawingee.

It was operated by the Tarrawingee Flux and Tramway Company from its opening until 1899, and thereafter by the Silverton Tramway Company. The line was lifted in 1936.
