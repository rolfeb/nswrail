Description: In 1936 the up-side station resided on a 50m platform. At the north end was a goods siding with shed and jib crane. Further up the line was a water tank and column. On the opposite side was a grain siding, 100m grain shed and stock yards. Further beyond that was a siding serving some silos and another grain shed. There was also a down-side triangle for turning locomotives; this was removed at a later stage before the line closed.
Current status: Platform remains. Two lever frames are visible. A silo is nearby.
