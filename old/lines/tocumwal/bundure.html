Bundure Railway Station

 Coonong North Yathong 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Tocumwal Branch
Location: Lat/long: (146.0084°, -35.1673°)
Distance: From Sydney: 654.420 km
From Narrandera: 70.94 km
History:
16-Sep-1884   Opened
17-Nov-1975   Moved site
Description: Opened in 1925 with a 90m platform on the down side. Opposite was a loop siding with wool loading platform and stock races.
Current status: The platform still holds a very faded name sign. The goods siding has been lifted, but the wool platform still lies directly opposite the platform.
