Finley Railway Station

 Curraghmohr Langunyah 
Main facility: Station
Status: Re-used
Lines: Tocumwal Branch
Location: Lat/long: (145.5730°, -35.6498°)
Distance: From Sydney: 743.440 km
From Narrandera: 159.96 km
History:
16-Sep-1898   Opened
unknown   Closed
Description: A 50m platform and station resided in the up side of the line. On the same side was a goods siding with goods shed, grain sheds and loading bank. On the opposite side was a stock siding and yards. Beyond that and directly opposite the station was an engine shed, carriage shed and turntable. By 1941 a set of silos had been erected to the south of the station. The engine facilities were removed at some stage before the closure of the line.
Current status: 2 goods platforms remain. The station building is being used as a community centre. 3 tracks remain in yard. Lever frames and scales are present. Silo visible down-line.
