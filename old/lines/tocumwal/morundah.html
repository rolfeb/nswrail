Morundah Railway Station

 New Park Widgiewa 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Tocumwal Branch
Location: Lat/long: (146.3041°, -34.9339°)
Distance: From Sydney: 613.830 km
From Narrandera: 30.35 km
History:
16-Sep-1884   Opened as Colombo
1-Oct-1889   Renamed Morundah
17-Nov-1975   Closed
Description: Opening in 1888 as Colombo Creek, this was quite a significant location. The station on the down side sat on a 90m platform. At the down end was a 20,000 gallon water tank. Behind the station was the station master's house, and 100m down the line was the gate house for the level crossing. On the up side was a loop siding and a goods siding, the latter serving a goods platform and shed.
Current status: No trace remains of the platform. A goods bank and silos still remain, as do 2 sidings.
