Widgiewa Railway Station

 Morundah Coonong 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Tocumwal Branch
Location: Lat/long: (146.2066°, -35.0511°)
Distance: From Sydney: 631.540 km
From Narrandera: 48.06 km
History:
16-Sep-1884   Opened
4-May-1975   Closed
Description: Opened in 1913 as a 34m platform on the down side. Opposite was a loop siding with loading bank.
Current status: A raised concrete platform and adjacent signal hut are in relatively good condition. 3 sets of levers are still present, although goods siding and loading bank have gone. The remains of the Up Home signal are [unusually] mounted on a telegraph pole.
