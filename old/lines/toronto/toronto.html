Toronto Railway Station

 Bowers Platform
Main facility: Station
Status: Re-used
Lines: Toronto Branch
Location: Lat/long: (151.5980°, -33.0154°)
Distance: From Sydney: 146.045 km
From Fassifern: 3.73 km
History:
7-Mar-1891   Opened
unknown   Closed
Description: The station comprised a platform with station building on the up side of the line, is a cutting below Victory Parade. At the down end of the start was a yard with a loop siding and two other sidings, one the latter serving a goods shed.
Current status: The platform and station building are still present, and are being used by the Lake Macquarie and District Historical Society. The station building and platform have been preserved exactly as they were when the line was operational. The Up Home signal is still present. The yard at the down end of the station is no longer present, being covered up by a park.
