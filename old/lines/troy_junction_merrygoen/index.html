Troy Junction - Merrygoen Line

  • History
  • Maps

This is a part of the northern cross-country line, joining Troy Junction (near Dubbo),

to the town of Merrygoen, on the Gwabegar branch. It is used for freight trains carrying wheat, and other traffic which wants to avoid the Main Western line over the Blue Mountains.
