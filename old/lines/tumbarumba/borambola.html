Borambola Railway Station

 Coreinbob Tarcutta 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Tumbarumba Branch
Location: Lat/long: (147.6753°, -35.2013°)
Distance: From Sydney: 562.200 km
From Wagga Wagga: 40.80 km
History:
14-May-1917   Opened
4-May-1975   Closed
Description: Opened with a station on an 85m platform on the up side of the line. Opposite were a goods siding with loading bank, goods shed and gantry crane. At the down end of the platform was a water tank. Further down the line was a stock siding serving stock yards.
Current status: A platform and shed are visible in a field, as well as a lever frame to the north.
