Humula Railway Station

 Sheahan Nurla 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Tumbarumba Branch
Location: Lat/long: (147.7605°, -35.4911°)
Distance: From Sydney: 604.600 km
From Wagga Wagga: 83.20 km
History:
14-May-1917   Opened
4-May-1975   Closed
Description: The station stood on an 85m down platform. A tank at the down end served water columns at each end of the platform. Opposite were a loop siding and a goods siding, the latter with a loading bank, goods shed and gantry crane. At the down end of the station was a triangle leading to the coal stage, and a stock siding and stock yards.
Current status: Visible from the road are a siding, 2 two-lever frames and the associated signals.
