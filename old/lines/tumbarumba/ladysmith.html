Ladysmith Railway Station

 RAAF Wagga Wagga Tamboolba 
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Tumbarumba Branch
Location: Lat/long: (147.5145°, -35.2064°)
Distance: From Sydney: 640.006 km
From Wagga Wagga: 118.61 km
History:
14-May-1917   Opened
4-May-1975   Closed
Description: Consisted of a station building on an 85m down-side platform. Opposite was a loop line with a goods siding serving a loading bank, goods shed and gantry crane. Further up the line was a stock siding with stock yard.
Current status: The station building and infrastructure is being preserved by the "Tumba Rail" group. Also visible are several lever frames, the goods shed and loading bank and signals. There is a silo at the north end of the yard.
