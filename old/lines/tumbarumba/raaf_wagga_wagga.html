RAAF Wagga Wagga Railway Station

 Forest Hill Ladysmith 
Main facility: Halt
Status: Closed
Lines: Tumbarumba Branch
Location: Lat/long: (147.4681°, -35.1573°)
Distance: From Sydney: 531.130 km
From Wagga Wagga: 9.73 km
Description: A halt used by trains chartered by the RAAF to transport cadets to and from the RAAF base, it was about 400m beyond Forest Hill. It had a loop but no protecting signals or platform. The siding could only hold seven 22ft wagons.
