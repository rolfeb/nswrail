History:
9-May-1921   Opened
unknown   Closed
Description: The station stood in an 83m platform on the down side of the line. Opposite the platform was a run-around loop and a goods siding and loading bank. At the down end of the station was a siding leading to the coal stage, turntable and engine shed. At the up end of the yard was a stock siding and stock yards.
Current status: Present are the goods platform, the remains of the yard and a water tank.
