Bongalong Railway Station

Status: Closed

Location: Lat/long: (148.1127°, -34.8183°)

History:
1887   Opened
Nov-1891   Platform added
4-May-1975   Closed
Description: An 8m platform made of wood and essentially serving 2-3 houses. It had a full cement sign with metal letters. The platform height was only 2 feet (0.6m) maximum. A ramp existed on the up side of the line while the platform itself was on the down side of the line. Two private level crossings were at the site and had metal gates across the track.
