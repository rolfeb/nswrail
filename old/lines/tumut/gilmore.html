Current status: Extensive yard with island platform with signal box and lever frame. Opposite the platform is a goods shed and several semaphore signals. At the east end of the yard is a rotting 3-track bridge. Beyond the bridge lies a large lever frame and a two-arm semaphore signal. To the west lies the junction of the Batlow branch.
