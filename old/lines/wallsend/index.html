Wallsend Branch

  • History

A colliery line which extended from the original Hanbury Junction south the the Wallsend, Wallsend Borehole and Elermore Collieries. The line left the Main North near the Maud Street bridge, running alongside the present-day roads of University Drive, Wilkinson Avenue, and Cowper Street.

This line was unusual for a colliery line in that it also featured a station at Wallsend.
