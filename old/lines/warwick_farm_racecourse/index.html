Warwick Farm Racecourse Branch

  • History

The Warwick Farm Racecourse line was a 1.6 km line from Warwick Farm to the nearby racecourse, owned by the Australian Jockey Club. The branch left the Main South near Warwick Farm station. It then crossed the Hume Highway at a level crossing before ending at the Racecourse station.

At one point, the line had a 200m Horse Dock platform, and facilities for storing up to 8 trains. The Branch was closed in 1991, with the last passenger training running on 18 August 1990.
