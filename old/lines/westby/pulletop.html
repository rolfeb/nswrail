Pulletop Railway Station

 Burrandana Westby 
Main facility: Platform
Status: Closed
Lines: Westby Branch
Location: Lat/long: (147.3791°, -35.4359°)
Distance: From Sydney: 585.100 km
From Jct (Westby Branch): unknown
History:
5-Aug-1925   Opened as Pullitop
8-May-1932   Renamed Pulletop
24-Jan-1952   Closed
Description: Opened with just a down side loading bank and siding. Later a 30m platform was added on the up side.
Current status: The concrete-faced loading bank still remains. The right of way is now used as an access track to a nearby property.
