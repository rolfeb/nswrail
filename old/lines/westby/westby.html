Westby Railway Station

 Pulletop
Main facility: Station
Status: Closed
Lines: Westby Branch
Location: Lat/long: (147.4172°, -35.4905°)
Distance: From Sydney: 591.620 km
From Jct (Westby Branch): unknown
History:
5-Aug-1925   Opened
24-Jan-1952   Closed
Description: Westby had a 70m down side platform with station building, as well as the usual goods siding and loading bank. As this was the end of the line, there was also a turntable, ash pit, water tank and loop siding.
Current status: The station building is still standing. Some restoration work appears to have taken place.
