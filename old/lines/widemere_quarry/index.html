Widemere Quarry Line

  • History

The Widemere Quarry Line was a private branch which served Widemere Quarry, in Sydney's west (just to the east of Prospect Reservoir).

The line was closed on 2/6/1945 and lifted soon after. As the line ran mainly along streets, subsequent development has removed all traces of it.
