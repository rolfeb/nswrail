Yanco - Griffith Line

  • History
  • Maps

When it was decided to build a line to Hillston (and eventually Roto),

construction started from Yanco, and proceeded to Hillston via the then terminus station of Griffith.

Passenger trains to Griffith still traverse this section rather than the more direct route via Temora, as there are more significant towns by this route.
