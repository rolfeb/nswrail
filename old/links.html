NSW Railway Links

This page contains links to other people's pages which have photos or information related the the NSW railway network. You should also check out the list of operators in NSW.

If you want to add a link to this page, let me know!

Discussions

RailPage Forum: NSW
The RailPage forum covering NSW issues.

Location-Specific

Corowa Station
Brian Evans has taken some photos of the station and yard at Corowa. These are being used as a toy museum and parkland respectively.
Kiama Station
Information and pictures from Kiama station (South Coast line).
Kywong Station
Clem Thompson's family were associated with Kywong station, on the Uranquinty-Kywong line.
Telegraph Point Station
A history of the North Coast line station, put together by the local community.
Yass Station
The station building at Yass is now a railway museum.
East Hills Line
Danny Mok's pages devoted to the East Hills line,in Sydney.
Kurrajong Line
Alan Aldrich has set up a great page on the history of the Richmond-Kurrajong section of the Richmond line.
SkiTube
The SkiTube is a rack rail line in the Snowy Mountains which carries skiers in the winter and tourists in the summer.
Valley Heights Locomotive Depot Heritage Museum
The former locomotive depot has been turned into a museum, preserving the buildings and railway heritage of the area.
Lithgow: State Mine Heritage Park and Railway
The State Mine Colliery Branch ran from Lithgow to the nearby State Mine. The site has been turned into a museum, with plans to operate trains on the branch.
Blue Mountains Area
This page has lots of information on railways in the Blue Mountains (Main West, just to the west of Sydney).
Lake Macquarie Area
Lake Macquarie Council have put up a number of historic photos of lines in their area (Main North, Belmont and Toronto branches).
South Maitland Mines
The Newcastle Regional Museum has put up a huge amount of historical information on mining in the South Maitland area. Although not specifically about railways, there is a lot of information about the various lines which existed in this area.
Zig Zag
A collection of photos taken around the famous Lithgow Zig Zag.

RailPage Australia: NSW electric archive

Urban Adventure: Rust on the Rails
This is a web site devoted to exploring some of our abandoned industrial sites. This section covers abandoned railways.
Ben Tulloch's page
Ben Tulloch has a page on NSW railways.
State Library of NSW
The State Library of NSW has set up PICMAN, a searchable image database with many hundreds of historic railway photos.
Picture Australia
Picture Australia has compiled indexes for several collections of photographs including the State Library's PICMAN database.
Andrew Young's page
Andrew has a number pictures from around NSW including a great collection of signalling and safeworking equipment and signal boxes.
Australian Railway Detail Photos
Run by Keiran Ryan, this site provides lots of rolling stock and infrastructure photos espcially for modellers.
Chris Nelson's Photos
Chris Nelson's photos.
