Profile Overview

MCC allows you to fill out a profile which tells people about you.  A number of fields are required, like name and e-mail others depend on the setup of MCC by your website administrator.

Editing Profile

To change your profile, click the "Profile" option in the menu.

Profile Options

- Password
Passwords have a default restriction of minimum 5 and maximum 15 long, but can be changed by your administrator, using a combination of characters and numbers is adviced. Both password fields must be the same, simply as a check that you typed it correctly. Default the password fields are empty filling them will change you password. If you don't want to change your password leave them empty. 
Example: xd8bill
- Name
This is your real or displayed name. This is what will be shown throughout MCC, in place of your username.
Example: John Doe
- Email Address
This must be valid to register, and things may be sent to this email address by the administrator, such a notification of a upgrade. It is also required if you ever wish to retreive a forgotton password.
Example: me@mydomain.com

Use the 'change profile' button to save your profile or 'delete user' to remove you self as a member. If you did not change anything you can use 'back' to go one page back.
