Photographic Theme - Track Diagrams

This page contains photos of track diagrams as found in many signal boxes.

Ariah Park (2004) The track diagram mounted above the lever frame. Geoff Allcock
Binnaway (1996) A close-up of the signal diagram. Rolfe Bozier
Boggabri (2001) The Boggabri track diagram. The station is situated below the "FRAME A" label. Malcolm Johnstone
Boorowa (1980) The diagram on the A lever frame, at the down end of the platform. Graeme Skeet

Broadmeadow (1979) The right hand side of the signal diagram in the Broadmeadow Signal Box. Graeme Skeet
Bulli (2004) The yard diagram for Bulli station. This is not the original board. Apparently the original board was stolen one night and the thief left a photograph of the original board and the Museum has reproduced this board from the photo. Kevin Jones
Bulli (2004) The yard diagram for the coal sidings. Kevin Jones
Camellia (1981) The line diagram in the Camellia Signal Box. The branch at the top is the short Sandown line. Graeme Skeet
Canowindra (2001) The Canowindra yard diagram. Mark Zanker
Canowindra (2001) The yard diagram and lever frame. Mark Zanker
Central (1978) One of the diagrams in Sydney West signal box. This one shows platforms 1 - 4, the main long distance and inter-state platforms. Graeme Skeet
Central (1970) The signal diagram for the Wells Street signal box. Chris Bentley
Chatswood (2004) A close-up of the track diagram. Chris Bentley
Coolac (2003) The signal diagram for Coolac has been preserved and is now in the Gundagai Railway Station. Kevin Jones

Crooble (2001) The fairly simple yard diagram in the staff hut. Malcolm Johnstone
